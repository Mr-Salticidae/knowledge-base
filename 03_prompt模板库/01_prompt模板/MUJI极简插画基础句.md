# MUJI 极简插画基础句

> 首次沉淀:2026-05-12(「星河如丝般顺滑」获奖图复盘)
> 主线作品来源:`03_prompt模板库/02_案例复盘/星河如丝般顺滑获奖图复盘.md`

---

## 一句话总结

**一组可复用的关键词组合**,能稳定锁定"MUJI / 长场雄 / 中村佑介早期"那条线的日系极简插画感,不会跑去当代漫画 / 韩系小红书壁纸 / instagram 风。

---

## 如何使用

✅ 适用主题:**情绪 / 意境 / 抽象触觉**类的人像(尤其撞车红海题材,用这套媒介差异化破局)
- "被星河浇灌"
- "孤独午后"
- "等一封信"
- "听海"

✅ 不适用:写实摄影、动作戏强、需要复杂场景叙事的题材

⚠️ **配合**:[[极简插画删表情律]]——表情词主动删干净

---

## 基础句模板

```
minimalist japanese illustration in the style of muji aesthetic,
[主体描述,简短一句],
drawn with extremely fine clean ink lines and flat muted color fills,
features rendered with absolute minimalism only two small dot eyes
a tiny soft pink mouth and a hint of cheek blush,
[姿态/动作,主要靠这里承载情绪],
[主要色调,4 色以内],
enormous negative space surrounding the figure,
composition with the [主体] positioned in the [角落位置],
restrained palette of [4 个具体色名],
no shading no gradients only flat clean colors,
fine textured paper feel,
[情绪锚 1-2 个 atmosphere 词],
contemporary japanese editorial illustration aesthetic
```

---

## 关键词四层锚(对应「告白复盘」方法论 B)

| 锚类 | 关键词 |
|---|---|
| **时代锚** | `contemporary japanese editorial illustration aesthetic` |
| **媒介锚** | `minimalist japanese illustration in the style of muji aesthetic` |
| **工艺锚** | `extremely fine clean ink lines` + `flat muted color fills` + `no shading no gradients only flat clean colors` + `fine textured paper feel` |
| **色调锚** | `restrained palette of [4 个具体色名]`(例:cream off-white / dusty lavender / soft indigo / faint rose) |

**四层全上,媒介纯度才稳**。少一层就有跑偏风险。

---

## 必须主动删的词(摄影术语污染)

参考 [[告白获奖图复盘]] 方法论 C,这些词在插画风格里**一律删干净**,否则会把画面拉回写实:

- ❌ `shot on medium format film`
- ❌ `cinematic`
- ❌ `hyperreal texture`
- ❌ `fine grain`
- ❌ `cinematic surrealism`
- ❌ `detailed rendering` / `intricate details`
- ❌ `fashion editorial` / `vogue editorial`
- ❌ 任何摄影器材/胶片型号词

---

## 配色推荐(可直接套)

| 情绪 | 4 色组合 |
|---|---|
| 静默温柔(星河) | cream off-white / dusty lavender / soft indigo / faint rose |
| 落寞清晨 | warm beige / pale gray / dusty olive / muted terracotta |
| 海边孤独 | off-white / pale sky blue / faded teal / sand beige |
| 山居秋意 | cream / muted moss green / soft umber / pale persimmon |

**全部低饱和、4 色以内**,不要用 instagram 那种高饱和粉紫渐变。

---

## 留白指令(必须显式写)

不能默认靠风格出留白,要写死:

```
enormous negative space surrounding the figure
composition with the [主体] positioned in the [lower right / lower left / right edge]
[主要元素] descending from upper area into the empty [背景色] background
```

把构图位置 + 留白方向都给死,MJ 才会真的留出大块负空间,而不是把元素填满画面。
